Our Story

A foundation built on compassion.

Harbor Haven Foundation is a compassionate, community-driven organization dedicated to providing safety, support, and hope to vulnerable individuals.

How We Started

A safe space for hope.

Founded by Aisha Aliyu, Harbor Haven was born out of a deep desire to create a safe space where people facing hardship...especially women, children, and those in crisis...can find refuge, care, and a second chance at life.

At Harbor Haven, we believe that everyone deserves a place where they feel protected, valued, and empowered. We work to provide not only immediate support such as shelter and essential resources, but also long-term opportunities that help individuals rebuild their lives with dignity and confidence.

Our Focus Areas
  • • Providing safe shelter and care
  • • Supporting vulnerable individuals and families
  • • Promoting education and personal development
  • • Empowering people to achieve independence and stability

We are committed to restoring hope and transforming lives, one person at a time. Through compassion, integrity, and community support, Harbor Haven Foundation continues to be a place where healing begins and brighter futures are built.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped us.

2018
Foundation Established

Harbor Haven registers as an NGO in Abuja, opens its first community outreach point in Garki.

2019
First Shelter Opens

A 12-bed emergency shelter for women and children opens in Wuse 2, serving 80 individuals in its first year.

2021
Mental Health Program Launch

Partnering with certified counselors, we launch free mental health support sessions across 4 community centers.

2023
4,000 Lives Milestone

Harbor Haven surpasses 4,000 individuals directly supported ...a milestone marked with a community celebration.

2024
National Recognition

Recognized by the Federal Ministry of Humanitarian Affairs as a trusted implementing partner for GBV response.

What We Do

Programs built around real needs.

At Harbor Haven Foundation, we are committed to improving lives by providing support, protection, and empowerment to vulnerable individuals and communities. Our work is centered around compassion, dignity, and creating lasting impact. Through our programs, we address critical needs and help people build safer, healthier, and more stable futures.

01
Mental Health Support

We recognize that mental well-being is essential for a healthy and fulfilling life. Many individuals facing hardship also struggle with emotional and psychological challenges.

  • • Emotional support and guidance
  • • Awareness programs on mental health
  • • Safe spaces where individuals can share their experiences
  • • Referrals to professional counseling when needed
Our goal is to break stigma, promote healing, and ensure that no one suffers in silence.
02
Basic Education Programs

Education is a powerful tool for transformation. We are dedicated to ensuring that vulnerable children and individuals have access to basic education.

  • • Literacy and numeracy classes
  • • Learning support for out-of-school children
  • • Educational resources and materials
  • • Skills development for self-reliance
We aim to empower individuals with knowledge and skills that open doors to better opportunities.
03
Women and Children Protection

Protecting women and children is at the heart of our mission. We work to ensure their safety, dignity, and well-being.

  • • Supporting victims of abuse and neglect
  • • Providing a safe and secure environment
  • • Advocacy against violence and exploitation
  • • Raising awareness on rights and protection
We strive to create a society where every woman and child feels safe, valued, and protected.
04
Community Outreach

We believe that strong communities create lasting change. Through our outreach programs, we extend support directly to those in need.

  • • Community support initiatives
  • • Distribution of essential items
  • • Awareness campaigns on health, safety, and well-being
  • • Engagement with local communities to identify needs
We work closely with communities to bring hope, support, and practical assistance where it is needed most.
